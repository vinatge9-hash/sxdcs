Our Story

Brew & Beans began as a simple dream: to craft exceptional coffee in a space that feels like home. We partner with small-batch roasters and prioritize ethical sourcing, freshness, and community. Our baristas bring warmth, knowledge, and a love for standout flavors to every cup.

Meet the Team

Passionate baristas and roasters who obsess over grind size, extraction, and pour technique to deliver consistency and joy in every cup.

Sourcing & Roasting

We partner with ethical farms and roasters, selecting beans for balance, aroma, and nuance. Roasting is done in small batches to maximize freshness.
